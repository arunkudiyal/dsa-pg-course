ABOUT DATA SCIENCE

Data Science is the hottest trend of the 21st century. It is one of the most popular jobs which has topped the LinkedIn Emerging Jobs List for the last 3 years in a row. As the world embraces digitisation, Data Science emerges as one of the most vital professions for businesses and individuals alike.

Data Science has the potential to revolutionise industries in a way that is unprecedented. The pandemic in the last few years has increased the consumption and dependency on the Internet. Amidst this Data Science technologies have created massive ripples in terms of healthcare, banking, logistics etc. From small business owners to global MNCs, today everyone is dependent on Data Scientists and uses Data Science to solve complex business problems in the most innovative manner.

This course was designed in order to serve this industry demand and market need when it comes to Data Science. Our curriculum has been designed in a way that theoretical training goes hand-in-hand with practical training. With us, you will get the opportunity to work on real-life use cases, work on projects, assignments and build a solid portfolio.
